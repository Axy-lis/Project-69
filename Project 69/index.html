Campus Life

MPR(Multi-purpose room)

Was built in 1989

The MPR is the place where most, if not all, assemblies(Announcments, indoor lunch, etc.) and school events(Such as guest speakers, graduations, plays/skits, and the talent show) will occur. On days where there is severe and potentially dangerous weather outside, recess and lunch will be indoors in the MPR.

Library

Was built in 1963

The library has many learning materials and is open to kids during recess, lunch, and during class(if allowed by the teacher). It has many resources, ranging from books, to computers, to colored pencils. The library is a great place to learn about things you are curious about, both in and out of school.

Gymnasium

Was built in 1970

The gymnasium is where you will have your PE(physical education). Coach Jim or Coach Stevenson will usually be in the gym, so if you need to talk to them, the gym is the place to go. School games will also be played here.



school achievements

Inter School Debate On Environment Pollution
Won first prize in inter school debate on environment pollution

Our school won first place in the inter school debate on enviromental pollution. The group gave great facts and had solid reasons behind their argument. The trophy they one is on display in the shelf near the hall.

Inter School Football Competition
Came second in inter school football competition

Recently, our school has come in 2nd place in the inter school football competition. Our team has really pushed through this time, and took away not one, but 2 medals! One for coming in second place and the other for best teamwork.

Inter School Science Project Competition
Won second price in inter school Science Project competition

Our school won second place in the inter school science project competition. The team made a project to show the ecosystem of a jungle and even made a diorama to display the information visually.
